(##) __Ydrok__ --- ![](MAP_Ydrok_v1_Printable.png) Ydrok is known by many beings across the planes as a crossroads; a rare and twisted intertwining of dimensional forces which has, upon numerous occasions, changed the course of history entirely. This, as a whole, is what defines Ydrok’s story as world, and the stories of the people within it. From the harsh, untamed deserts of Hiszharra, to the corrupted jungles of Chi’Brraka, to the overwhelming magic and mystery of Zeflazan’s wilderness and the draconic ruins of Uragol, there are flora and fauna, and indeed, entire civilizations, that are acutely crafted by the fate of the land they happened to reside in. The cultures and continents of Ydrok are not mere leaves in the winds of destiny; they are also the works of mighty beings, the strongest and most cunning, who bend this world to their will, one piece at a time. There are the ten __runic deities__ who come from the polar opposite planes of alignment; the gods of the humans as they are often known. Then there is the __council of elements__ and their numerous kin, who discuss the fate of Ydrok atop the pinnacle of elements. These beings are the most commonly recognized religious figures across all races and cultures. The oldest and most individually powerful of Ydrok’s pantheons . . . (###) The Arbiters of the Apocalypse First, there was __Zorp__, the most ancient and evil of the lot; Victor of the Primal Wars, Commander of the Legion of Bones, and King of Flame and Darkness. Then there was __Sorak__; the Mistress of Mana, Empress of the Mantid, and Paragon to the Legion of Souls. Third came __Chir__ and __Kralk__, the reflections of chaos and law in the realm of fae, revered most by the perilous grippli. Finally, the most recent deity to appear on Ydrok, __Hiszhurr__; Liberator of the Humans, Challenger to Zorp, and bringer of a cold, bitter end. --- (###) These are the forces that have shaped
Ydrok and the people within it,
including you, bold adventurers.
(##) Will your life mark this world as those before it?
